Download BERT models publickly available in this folder.
Convert the Tensorflow models to Pytorch version and name it "pytorch_model.bin" (see huggingface's convert codes)
Then, place the "pytorch_model.bin" in corresponding model folder.
We used following BERT models in our study:
- bertbase_cased
- bioBERT/biobert_v1.1_pubmed
- mbert_cased
- KoBERT (skt coporation version)
